On the Subject of Resolving Incidents

“Extra~ Extra~ Nothing big happened, but it's still an extra!”

  • “You have an incident to resolve, character! The question is can you claim victory on the incident alive?”
  • There are three screens and one active spell LED that show the entire incident.
  • Each screen consists of the name of the character, the name of the incident, and 5 items in the inventory.
  • The character will have a Season that is represented by the coloring of its name.
Blue = WinterGreen = Spring
Yellow = SummerRed = Fall
  • Keywords in bold are referred to the appendix on the last page of the manual.

  • Upon resolving incidents, a forbidden incident does not occur. Meaning the character (that is a part of the same incident) and the incident’s boss cannot be the same. Example is on the appendix.

  • Begin at Step 1 to determine the character’s starting distance and range. Keep working through these steps to determine the incident’s boss and the applicable items until Step 5. Apply bonuses, if it applies.

  • Be aware of the Widget Expander Rule. The rule would be labeled as
    “8+ Widgets,” and explains what to do, if you have 8 or more widgets on the bomb. Such rule exist to prevent the high skew of win-lose ratio.
